## Boundary data

Production geometry is based on the Indonesia ADM2 dataset made available through geoBoundaries and the HDX COD-AB Indonesia package.

- Dataset: Indonesia - Subnational Administrative Boundaries / geoBoundaries IDN ADM2
- Publishing organizations: HDX/OCHA COD-AB and geoBoundaries
- Original source lineage reported by geoBoundaries: Badan Pusat Statistik (BPS - Statistics Indonesia), World Food Programme, OCHA ROAP
- Boundary year represented: 2020
- License reported by geoBoundaries: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Intergovernmental Organisations (CC BY 3.0 IGO)
- HDX package license title: Creative Commons Attribution for Intergovernmental Organisations (CC BY-IGO)
- geoBoundaries API: https://www.geoboundaries.org/api/current/gbOpen/IDN/ADM2/
- HDX package: https://data.humdata.org/dataset/cod-ab-idn

The MIT License for this project does not apply to third-party boundary data.
